How to Play

Solve the checkmate puzzle in 5 attempts or less!

  • Enter 5 moves, white moves first(puzzle is a checkmate in 3 white moves and 2 black moves)
  • The move format is Piece → Column → Row for example ♖b1 = move Rook to the square b1
  • Click a piece (♔♕♖♗♘♙), then a column (a-h), then a row (1-8). Once all 5 moves are entered, click Submit
  • Green = correct sequence, Orange = part of sequence is correct but not all
